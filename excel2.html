Chocolates Sales Perfomance Dashboard (2021-2022)

This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive visualization of Chocolate's sales performance for the years 2021 and 2022. It includes key metrics such as:

  • Total Revenue: Track the overall income generated from chocolate sales during these two years.
  • Sales Growth: Analyze the month-over-month sales growth to understand trends and identify peak periods.
  • Top-Performing Products: Highlight the best-selling chocolates, allowing stakeholders to focus on products that drive the most revenue.
This dashboard enables stakeholders to easily assess the company's performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions to optimize sales strategies and product offerings.
